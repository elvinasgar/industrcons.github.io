01 — About

Construction &
Engineering Platform

IndustrCons is a professional community platform designed to bring together specialists, employers, HR professionals and job seekers active in the construction and engineering sector.

Our platform's core purpose is to build professional connections within the sector, share vacancies, promote knowledge exchange and expand career opportunities.

Mission
Unite people in the construction and engineering sector on a single platform to develop employment opportunities, professional connections and knowledge sharing.
Vision
To become the largest construction and engineering professionals network in Azerbaijan and the broader region.
What is IRE-3?
Industry Ready Engineer — a concept for engineers ready for the sector. A program framework created to develop professionals who bridge theory and practice in the field.
